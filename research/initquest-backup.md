# InitQuest — Warroom V1 Winner (Backup Reference)

**Score:** 7.88/10
**Track:** Gaming & Consumer

## Concept
Gamified onboarding RPG that teaches Initia's platform through interactive quests. 4 core quests each demonstrating a native feature (auto-signing, interwoven bridge, .init usernames, Move objects). Players earn XP, badges (Move NFTs), and levels.

## Why It Won V1
- Only idea using ALL THREE native features as core mechanics
- Zero competitors in onboarding gamification
- Target users exist in Discord (confused new devs)
- Meta-narrative: "we built what every other team needed"

## Weaknesses (Why Builder Rejected)
- Only 4 quests — too thin for a "quest platform"
- All V1 ideas were gaming-biased — didn't explore DeFi or AI tracks
- Ideas felt incremental, not exciting enough
